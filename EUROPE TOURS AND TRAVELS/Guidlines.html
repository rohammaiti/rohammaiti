Tourist Guidlnes


  1. Before visiting any place, read rules for incoming international passengers, visa and passport guidlines, currency exchange and update them if necessary.
  2. Check out near by health centres, hospitals present near hotels and health schemes about the respective country. Make sure to check with Covid-19 rules and regultions on arrival.
  3. Know your nearby police outposts and keep their contacts in case of emergency.
  4. If you are new to a place we strongly recommend you to hire a local tourist guide as they will be helping you to move through the city uninterupptedly.
  5. Try to know the local language that is spoken in the area. It is advisable to hire a translator if necessary.
  6. Always stock your wallets and e-payment portals with adequate money.
  7. Always check for the current news and updates of the area which you are visiting.
