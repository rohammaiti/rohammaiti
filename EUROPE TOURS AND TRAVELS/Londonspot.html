Top things to do in London


While visiting London, some of the things that you must include in your tour plans are listed below-

  • Boating down the river Thames is one of a great experience. You can take motor boat or any cruise to sail down the river
  • You can take a double decker or a tour bus to roam around the London city in night. The nights of London is a must to experience with light shows,
    carnivals, concerts and all.
  • Do visit Borough market once. The food-focused Borough Market is effectively three markets in one (with restaurants, bars and shops interspersed throughout).
    Three Crown Square features large producers and merchants while Green Market has smaller, specialty ones.
  • Go through palaces, abbeys, parks and some war memorials located in the city.
